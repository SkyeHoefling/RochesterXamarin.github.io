Process.

Design

Rochester Xamarin Consulting works with each client as they design their new app. Mobile app design is an iterative process where the design team listens to what the clients needs until there is a working prototype.

Development

Demos will be provided on a weekly or bi-weekly basis, which keeps the client informed of how features are implemented. This gives the client an opportunity to provide critical feedback as the team goes through their iterative development cycles.

Release

All deployments to the various mobile stores is handled in house which is one less thing for you to worry about. Automated 1-click deploys make it easy to update the app and get pre-release builds in your hands faster.

Support

When issues arise, our support team will be standing by to provide you with assistance. The customer portal provides enterprise level support for any size of application.

Usability

Enterprise apps can get copmlicated very quickly, which makes them not very useful if the users can't use them. Our apps take all of this complex data into the design so the users can easily navigate the data and perform useful tasks with the data.

Reporting and Charting

Custom report generation and charts at the palm of your hand.

Real-Time Data

Instant Message

Hidden Features.

Authentication & User Profiles

Authentication systems allow users to log in to the app and gives the app ability to assign different user roles. Any complex app will require some level of Authentication and Authorization systems.

Web Services

A RESTful Web Service is crucial in making a powerful distributed app that retrieves and stores large amount of data in a database.

Database

Using a local or distributed database allows an app to store complex data objects.

Offline Support

Is it common your app will be used in dead zones or with limited connectivity? Offline support enables your app to work no matter where you are and sync back once you are back online.

Security

When good security practices are followed, the users will never know because it never becomes a problem.
